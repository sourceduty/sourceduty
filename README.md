Alex will be persistently maintaining Sourceduty and it's creative works without retiring. Sourceduty owns over 200 x 3D model assets and over 300 x GPT assets. This company is capable of producing and managing much more and it will be expanding into the deep future of the internet. Over the next decade, Sourceduty will continue to evolve, expanding its offerings and influence in the digital world.
